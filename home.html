FAQs

Boarding school is typically suitable for students aged 12 and above. This age allows students to develop independence, self-discipline, and academic readiness for the residential learning environment.

Sending kids to boarding school can be a valid choice for some families, offering structured education and personal development. However, it depends on individual circumstances, as it may affect family bonds and socialization. Careful consideration of the child's needs and preferences is crucial to make an informed decision.

Boarding schools can provide a structured environment for academic and personal growth, fostering independence and discipline. However, they may lead to feelings of isolation and detachment from family, impacting social development. It depends on individual circumstances and preferences.

To choose the best boarding school in Dehradun, consider factors such as academic reputation, faculty qualifications, infrastructure, extracurricular activities, safety measures, and alumni success. Visit schools, talk to current students and parents, and research online reviews. Make sure the school aligns with your child's needs and goals.
